Peihuan Ni

I am an incoming Ph.D. student (pre-admitted) at the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology, School of Computer Science, Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Chen Tian. My research primarily focuses on distributed systems for training and inference of large models.

Currently, I am a master's student at the School of Artificial Intelligence, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), advised by Prof. Jian Cheng and Assistant Prof. Gang Li, at the Efficient Intelligent Computing and Learning Lab (CLab), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I focus on efficient inference architectures.

Previously, I received my B.S. degree from the College of Electronic Science and Engineering at Jilin University (JLU).

Research Interests

My research interests include AI Infrastructure and distributed systems for the training and inference of large models. I am also interested in efficient inference architectures for Large Language Models and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, as well as Hardware Accelerators and Embodied Artificial Intelligence.
