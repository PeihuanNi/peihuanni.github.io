Peihuan Ni

I'm a master's student at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), School of Artificial Intelligence, Beijing, China, advised by Prof. Jian Cheng and Assistant Prof. Gang Li, at the Efficient Intelligent Computing and Learning Lab (CLAB). I received my B.S. degree from Jilin University (JLU), College of Electronic Science and Engineering in 2024. My research interests include Large Language Models, Hardware Accelerators, and Embodied Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on efficient inference.
